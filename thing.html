Things to Do in Dalhousie

Paragliding in Dalhousie

Fly Amidst the Clouds

Paragliding is quite a popular activity in Dalhousie. Kalatop and Khajjiar are some of the best spots to enjoy this activity.

Suggested Time: 2 to 3 hours

Dining at Café Dalhousie

Dig into Yummy Food

One of the most famous restaurants in Dalhousie, Café Dalhousie serves delicious vegetarian and non-vegetarian delicacies.

Suggested Time: 1 to 2 hours

Camping in Dalhousie

Experience the Rugged Outdoors

Dalhousie is a great place for camping. Kalatop and Daikund Peak are ideal spots for this adventure.

Suggested Time: 1 to 2 days

A Visit to Rang Mahal

Shop For Hand-Made Goods

Built in the 18th century, Rang Mahal reflects a mix of Mughal and British architecture and is perfect for shopping handmade goods.

Suggested Time: 1 hour

Shopping at Gandhi Chowk

Score Local and Tibetan Wares

If you’re looking for authentic Tibetan wares, Gandhi Chowk is a charming spot for shopping.

Suggested Time: 1 to 2 hours
