LIST OF AMAZING TREKS IN DALHOUSIE

Manimahesh Lake Trek

The trek takes 7-8 days, reaching an altitude of 13,500 ft. Includes food, camping, and activities for Rs 15,000.

Bara Bhangal Trek

This challenging trek is for 10 days, reaching a height of 15,305 ft. Includes all meals for Rs 25,000.

Sach Pass

A popular 7-day trek, set at 14,500 ft, offers a cultural and adventurous experience. Cost: Rs 14,000.

Kalatop to Khajjiar

A short 4-6 hour trek through the wildlife sanctuary to the "Mini Switzerland of India". Entry fee: Rs 250.

Dainkund to Jot Trek

An easy 3-4 hour trek from Dalhousie's highest peak. Ideal for first-time trekkers. Cost: Rs 850.
