Things to Do

Dalhousie boasts a variety of activities, from exploring colonial-era architecture to boating on the serene Chamera Lake. Visitors can enjoy local markets, savor Himachali cuisine, or simply soak in the tranquil ambiance of this charming hill station.

HIKING ESSENTIALS

Hike at Dalhousie!

Dalhousie is a hiker’s paradise with trails that lead through dense forests, verdant meadows, and picturesque hilltops. The Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary and Dainkund Peak are popular spots, offering breathtaking views and a chance to connect with nature. These trails are perfect for beginners and experienced hikers alike.

Resorts for Staycation

Dalhousie offers charming resorts that blend comfort with natural beauty. From cozy cottages with stunning valley views to luxurious retreats with top-notch amenities, these resorts cater to relaxation and rejuvenation.
